Overview

Purpose

Balkanwatch will provide simplified news recaps, expert analysis, and timely updates to our audience.

Audience

The intended audience for the website includes residents of the Balkan region, local businesses, community organizations, and individuals interested in regional news.

Content

Home page

The home page of the Balkan Regional News website An engaging hero section with a compelling headline and visually appealing image.A concise overview of BalkanWatch's offerings and the benefits of subscribing to our free newsletter.A prominent call-to-action (CTA) button or form for users to subscribe to the newsletter.A showcase of highlighted news articles with captivating headlines and brief summaries.

About page

The about page will provide users with more information about BalkanWatch, its mission, and its approach to simplifying news and providing expert analysis focused on the Balkans region. It should include:A brief introduction to BalkanWatch's purpose and goals.An overview of the team behind BalkanWatch and their expertise.An explanation of the unique value proposition offered by BalkanWatch.Contact information for users to reach out for inquiries or feedback.
