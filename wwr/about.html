About Us

Our History

We have been providing exceptional rafting adventures since 1985. From our humble beginnings as a small family-owned business, we have grown into a renowned rafting company.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences while prioritizing safety, customer satisfaction, and environmental conservation.

Our Values

At Wild Waters Rafting, we value teamwork, respect for nature, customer-centricity, and continuous improvement. These values guide everything we do.
